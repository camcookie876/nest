Privacy Policy

At Camcookie Nest, your privacy matters. We collect only the minimal data needed to run this site:

  • Content & Profiles: Stored in data/data.json. No external databases or third-party storage services.
  • Guest Preferences: Saved locally via localStorage.
  • Images & Files: Embedded as base64 in data.json.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, analytics, or advertising trackers.
  • Security: Admin actions, moderation logs, and user sessions are protected and only accessible by the site owner.

You have full control: you can clear your local data anytime from your browser’s settings. Thank you for trusting Camcookie Nest with your stories, code, and creativity.
