Community Guidelines

Camcookie Nest is a safe, inclusive place. All users agree to:

  • Respect one another. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
  • Keep content appropriate. Adult or sensitive material must carry a warning.
  • No sharing of personal data (passwords, addresses, etc.).
  • No hacking or malicious code. Projects detected as hacking attempts will be removed and accounts suspended.
  • Follow AI warnings. If a content warning pops up, respect it before proceeding.
  • Report issues. Use the “Report” button on any post that violates guidelines.

Violations may lead to removal of content, suspension of accounts, and device blocking. Thank you for helping keep Camcookie Nest cozy and safe!
